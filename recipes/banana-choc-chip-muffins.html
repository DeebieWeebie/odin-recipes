Banana Chocolate Chip Muffins

Moist and Tender Vegan Banana Chocolate Chip Muffins

This recipe makes the most delicious banana chocolate chip muffins. Nobody will know they are vegan! They have a crispy top yet are tender and moist on the inside. If desired, you can also leave out the chocolate chips for plain banana muffins. Also optional, you can add walnuts.

Making these is super simple! Start by preheating your oven to 350°F and lining a muffin tin with muffin papers. You will need two bowls. One is for the dry ingredients (white flour, baking soda, and salt), and one is for the wet ingredients plus sugar (sugar, oil, bananas, vanilla extract, and non-dairy milk or non-dairy yogurt).

You simply put the dry ingredients in one bowl, whisk together the sugar with the wet ingredients in the other bowl, and then combine the two once your oven is preheated! Gently fold in the chocolate chips and or walnuts last and scoop the batter into the muffin cups. PRO TIP: Putting batter into the tin is very tidy if you use an ice cream scoop. Viola, it's time to pop them in the oven. Bake for 20 to 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Ingredients

    Bowl One (dry ingredients)

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1½ teaspoons baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt

    • Bowl Two (wet ingredients)

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ⅓ cup canola oil
  • 4 ripe bananas, mashed
  • ¼ cup non-dairy milk or non-dairy yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

    • Reserved to Add Last:

  • 1 cup non-dairy semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup walnuts

Steps

    Bowl One (dry ingredients)

  1. Sift the flour, measure, and place it into the dry ingredient bowl.
  2. Add the baking soda and salt to the flour.
  3. Stir with a fork until well blended.

    4. Bowl Two (wet ingredients)

  4. Place the sugar into the wet ingredient bowl 2.
  5. Add the canola oil and whisk the sugar and oil until well blended.
  6. Add the well mashed bananas.
  7. Add the non-dairy milk or non-dairy yogurt and vanilla extract.
  8. Mix well.

    9. Wait Until Oven is Preheated to 350°F

  9. Line your muffin tin with muffin baking cup papers.
  10. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients.
  11. Stir with a rubber spatula until just blended.
  12. Fold in the chocolate chips and walnuts (if desired).
  13. Using an ice cream scoop, drop batter into lined muffin tin cups.
  14. Place the muffin tin in the oven and bake for 20 to 30 minutes (or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean).
  15. Let freshly baked muffins cool for 5 minutes in the tin and then transfer to a wire rack to finish cooling.

Enjoy!

This recipe makes 12 muffins.
